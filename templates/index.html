WIKIBOOKEY

Customize Your Own Book Based on Wikipedia Content in 3 Steps

Step 1: Choose A Related Article

You can pick the topic of your choice and WIkiBookey will effectively aggregate the top topics that are related from Wikipedia to your search into one page so that you can be more focused on what you want to accomplish in a shorter time.

Step 2: Manage Your Book Chapters

Here you are able to pick and choose the topics and place them in your preferred order, just like how you would write your own book.

Step 3: Choose a Style and Generate Your Book

Unleash your creative prowess with the colorful templates that come with options to change fonts, color and upload your favorite photo for your cover page. When all those are done, click “print” and you will find yourself owning a brand new book!
